Dear Mr/Miss HR

Thank you for your time and your great professional accomplishment which bring you to this website. Sincerely Thank you for visiting.

I know it is hard for you to choose the right person in a huge amount of resumes, so for all the purpose of this website is to answer a question

“why do you need me?”

At the very beginning of my study, A subject called ‘Organizational Dialogue’ gave me the concepts and ideas about effective communication. It helps me to establish my own communication skills - Humour, Empathies, and Encourage.

Like the Solver function in Excel :), I’d love to solve all kinds of issues or problems by a logical mind and a practical approach. It is not only about me feel gratifying or self-actualization but also to acquire new knowledge through the problems and improve myself. I am a skilled person who loves to solve problems that I undertake or situation that I am presented with.

It is hard to say that I am the best fit guy for your position but I dare say that I will be valuable for you and your company.

Looking forward to seeing you!

Sincerely
