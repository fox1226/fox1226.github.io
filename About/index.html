The Idealist 理想主義者

Posted by 张 骏晓 （Fox） on May 20, 2017

「我们坚持一件事情，并不是因为这样做了会有效果，而是坚信，这样做是对的。」
——瓦茨拉夫·哈维尔



一直很敬佩「瓦茨拉夫·哈维尔」，一位有名的理想主義者，所以將他的名言放到了文章的開頭。這位捷克前總統以作為不同政見者而聞名，哈維爾主張人性的彰顯與真實的生活，1979年以「顛覆共和國」名義入獄，卻在1989年捷克舉行的第一次真正的民主選舉中當選為捷克斯洛伐克聯邦共和國總統。

我相信一个真正的「理想主义者」对人性，社会的本质，生命的本质有着充足的理解和坚信，所以即使遇到再大的问题都决不放弃。這種信念不僅僅是樂觀，而是相信自己的選擇。

所以一直以作為「理想主義者」而努力著，跳出世間的條條框框，在冷静思考后，选择一条虽然困难但值得奋斗的路，然后坚定不移的走下去。


寫於丁酉年

仲夏之夜






“Hope is definitely not the same thing as optimism. It is not the conviction that something will turn out well, but the certainty that something makes sense, regardless of how it turns out.”
——Václav Havel

I always admire Václav Havel as a well-known idealist, So I write his sentence at the top of this article. The former president of Czech Republic is famous as a political dissident. Havel stands for humanitarianism and anti-consumerism. However, his political activities brought him under the surveillance of the government. In 1979, Havel was arrested by the incumbent government. Nevertheless, in 1989, Havel became the president of Czech Republic thought the first Czech democratic election. He brings democracy to his country as he believes.

I believe an Idealist must have a fully understand and faith for the essence of humanity, society and life. So they never quit when facing obstacles. The faith is not only about optimism, but the beliefs of their own choice.

For years, the philosophy of idealism is motivating me. I am trying to jump out of the box, trying to think dispassionately. So I can find a hard but meaningful way of life and then implement my choice.
